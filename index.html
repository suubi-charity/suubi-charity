About Us

Suubi Charity Foundation is dedicated to supporting abandoned children and underprivileged families. Through shelter, education, nutrition, and healthcare, we aim to restore hope, provide care, and create opportunities for a better future.

Our Programs

Child Shelter & Care

Providing safe homes, nutritious meals, emotional support, and a loving environment for abandoned and vulnerable children.

Education & Skills Training

Supporting children's education with school supplies, scholarships, and life skills training for long-term empowerment.

Support for Needy Families

Helping families with food, healthcare, and income-generating projects to reduce poverty and improve quality of life.
